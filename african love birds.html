Are you aware that lovebirds are parrots? Yes, they are parrots, though relatively small ones

They are members of the Psittaciformes order, which includes all parrots. Their bill is hooked, and their feet are zygodactyl (two toes point forward and two-point backward).

A lovebird is one of the smaller parrot species available as a pet, but this bird is curious and always moving.

Lovebirds are known for the loving, attentive bond they form with their partners, as their name suggests.
